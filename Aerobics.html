Aerobics Classes

Aerobic activity is anything that increases your heart rate, uses large muscle groups, and can be sustained for a long period of time. This includes, but is not limited to, activities such as walking, jogging, cycling or swimming.

  • Interval training
  • Strength exercises
  • Flexibility and stretching
